Discount >50% thường là dự toán cao bất thường.Positive discount = won below budget. Small negative discount (1–15%) = normal in VN procurement. Discount >50% typically indicates an unusually high budget estimate.

Weights: Q2: A=40%/B=30%/C=30% · Q3: A=15%/B=42%/C=43% · Q4: A=5%/B=47%/C=48%
